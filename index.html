About

You are in Salesfake! This is a sandbox environment intended to mimic Salesforce. You will be using Salesforce a lot here at Upstart!

Salesforce is a powerful tool for managing customer relationships, but it can be confusing. This "offline version" will allow you to get the feel of navigating Salesforce without risking unexpected customer contact. Even if you click on the wrong thing, you'll still be ok!

Most of your core work will be done through queues. Queues can be accessed via the links at the top of the page.

The Unassigned, Income Verification, and Finisher links will present you with buttons, which will assign you to a Funding Agreement.

Funding Agreeements are Salesforce pages containing the details of a loan application. In a live environment, you will also access Product via Funding Agreements.

The Case queue link contains buttons which will assign you to a Case. Cases contain customer email interactions, as well as complaints. Cases are vital to our customer experience (and, of course, part of the job!).

The Auto_Loans link is a bit different - it displays a list of funding agreements rather than buttons. Currently, this is how our auto loans are assigned: manually by selecting from a list. In Salesforce, all funding agreements can be selected this way regardless of product type.

Salesfake is intended to look and feel like Salesforce, but it does not have all of the same functionality. Please rely on your manager, resource guides, leads, and other sources of truth for updates and direction on how Salesforce should be used.

We'd love for this to be comprehensive, but Salesforce is a $150 billion company, and Jordan built this in like a month.
